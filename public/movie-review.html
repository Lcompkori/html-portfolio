Movie Review by Leanne

Movies Seen in 2023

The Black Demon

Review:
2 out of 5.
The idea of the plot was fun (a giant shark attacking characters in the movie). However, most of the action was in the last 30 minutes of the movie, and it was very anti-climactic.

The Little Mermaid

Review:
3 out of 5
The movie live action movie was pretty good overall. For the most part it compared to the cartoon version, following the plot closely. I enjoyed the real-life representation of the characters (Flounder as a fish, Sebastian as a crab, etc.) My two main criticisms is changing Sebastian's story line to not include his role as the musical director, and that Arial forgets that she is supposed to kiss Prince Eric by the end of the third day.

Sebastian as a maestro made the song Under the Sea make more sense, as he was able to produce a song with the various sea creatures on the fly.

Arial forgetting that she was supposed to kiss Prince Eric as part of the deal with losing her voice, ended up making her seem a little too ditzy and less interested in the Prince as her love interest.

Law Abiding Citizen

Review:
5 out of 5!
The plot was complex with a lot of twists. The characters and their development was great. I found myself constantly changing who I was routing for throughout the movie.
